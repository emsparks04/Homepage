How I Use Product:

I usually apply this lip liner before the last step in my makeup routine, which for me is mascara. Then once Im done with my mascara I will peel it off and grab a qtip and get it a little wet and wipe off some of the lip stain as well as blend it into the middle part of my lip. This stuff last all night!
